Breakfast Potatoes (Yukon Gold)

Freezer-friendly par-baked breakfast potatoes — crisp outside, fluffy inside.

← Back to Home
Batch: 4 lb Par-bake: 425°F / 220°C Freezer-friendly Cook from frozen

Ingredients

  • 4 lb Yukon Gold potatoes
  • 2 tbsp cornstarch
  • 3 tbsp canola or vegetable oil
  • 1 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 1/2 tsp paprika
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • Optional: 1/2 tsp sugar, 1/2 tsp MSG

Instructions

1) Cut

  1. Dice potatoes into 1/2–3/4 inch cubes.
  2. Rinse until the water runs mostly clear.

2) Parboil

  1. Boil potatoes in salted water for 4–5 minutes.
  2. Drain and let steam-dry for 5–10 minutes.

3) Season

  1. Toss potatoes with oil first.
  2. Mix cornstarch + all dry seasonings together, then coat potatoes evenly.

4) Par-bake (for freezing)

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. Spread potatoes on a sheet pan in a single layer.
  3. Bake 20–25 minutes, flipping once, until light golden (not fully cooked/crisped).

5) Freeze

  1. Cool completely.
  2. Freeze on a tray 1–2 hours until firm.
  3. Transfer to freezer bags (label + date).

Cook From Frozen

  • Air fryer: 400°F (200°C), 12–16 minutes, shake halfway
  • Oven: 425°F (220°C), 20–30 minutes, flip once
