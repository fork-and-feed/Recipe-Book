Peti​t Pains Fourrés

From: Jocelyne — savory stuffed buns with a rich, simmered meat filling.

← Back to Home
Prep: 15 min Simmer: 60 min Total: ~75 min Serves: 8–10

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 lbs ground pork or veal
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1/2 cup chili sauce
  • 1 can Campbell's Chicken Gumbo soup
  • 1 tbsp mustard
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Small buns (for stuffing)

Instructions

  1. Cook onions
    In a large pan, sauté the chopped onions until softened.
  2. Brown the meat
    Add the ground meats. Season with salt and pepper. Cook until fully browned.
  3. Combine
    Stir in chili sauce, chicken gumbo soup, and mustard.
  4. Simmer
    Let the mixture simmer gently for 1 hour, stirring occasionally, until thickened.
  5. Stuff
    Let the mixture cool slightly, then stuff into small buns.
  6. Serve
    Optional: bake stuffed buns until warmed and lightly toasted before serving.

Notes

  • Great for parties — make the filling ahead and rewarm before stuffing.
  • If the mixture gets too thick while simmering, add a small splash of water or broth.
