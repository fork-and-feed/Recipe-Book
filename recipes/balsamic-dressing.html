Balsamic Dressing

Quick 2:1 oil-to-vinegar dressing with Dijon for a stable, tangy emulsion.

← Back to Home
Prep: 5 min Cook: 0 min Total: 5 min Yield: ~3/4 cup

Ingredients

  • 6 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 tbsp vinegar (balsamic, red wine, or white wine)
  • 1 tbsp Dijon mustard
  • 1 tbsp sugar (or honey / maple syrup)
  • 1/4 tsp salt & pepper
  • 1–2 tsp dry spice (garlic and/or roasted red peppers)

Preparation

  1. Whisk or Shake
    Combine all ingredients and whisk (or shake in a jar) until emulsified.
  2. Taste Test
    With a 2:1 ratio, vinegar “zing” and sweetness are more pronounced—adjust to taste.

Notes

  • Dijon helps the dressing stay blended—especially with less oil.
  • Store in the fridge; shake again before using.
