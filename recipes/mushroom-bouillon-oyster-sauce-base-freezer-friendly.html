Mushroom Bouillon “Oyster Sauce” Base (Freezer-Friendly)

Freezer-ready umami sauce base (best frozen unthickened, then thickened fresh while cooking).

← Back to Home
Yield: 1 cup Prep: 5 min Simmer: 3 min Freezer-friendly

Ingredients (Base)

  • 4 mushroom bouillon tablets (Aurora)
  • 1 cup (250 ml) boiling water
  • 6 tbsp soy sauce (or tamari)
  • 4 tbsp brown sugar
  • 1 tbsp molasses or 2 tbsp dark soy sauce (for color/depth)
  • 2 tsp rice vinegar (optional, for balance)
  • 1 tsp sesame oil (optional)

Make the Base

  1. Dissolve tablets: Crush the 4 tablets and whisk into 1 cup boiling water until fully dissolved.
  2. Build flavor: Stir in soy sauce, brown sugar, molasses (or dark soy), and optional vinegar/sesame oil.
  3. Simmer: Simmer 2–3 minutes to blend, then cool completely.

Freeze (1 Cup)

Portion into:

  • 1 tbsp cubes (you’ll get ~16 cubes), or
  • 2 tbsp cubes (you’ll get ~8 cubes)

Freeze until solid, then transfer to a labeled freezer bag/container.

To Use (Thicken Fresh)

  1. Melt the amount you need in a pan and bring to a gentle simmer.
  2. Add slurry and simmer until glossy (15–30 seconds):

Slurry Ratios

  • Per 1 tbsp base: 1/8 tsp cornstarch + 1/8 tsp cold water
  • Per 1/4 cup base (4 tbsp): 1/2 tsp cornstarch + 1/2 tsp cold water
  • For the full 1 cup base: 2 tsp cornstarch + 2 tsp cold water

Whisk slurry in and simmer 15–30 seconds until thickened and glossy.

Storage

Freeze for 2–3 months for best flavor.
