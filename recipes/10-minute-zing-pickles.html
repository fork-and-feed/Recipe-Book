10-Minute “Zing” Pickles

Crunchy, bright quick pickles designed to be eaten immediately — perfect with rich, savory meats.

← Back to Home
Prep: 10 min Rest: 10 min Total: 20 min Serves: 2–4

Ingredients

  • 2–3 mini (Persian) cucumbers, sliced into thin coins (or ~4 inches of English cucumber)
  • 2 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1/2 tsp sesame oil
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • Optional heat: pinch of red pepper flakes or a dash of chili oil

Method

  1. Slice: Keep the cucumbers thin — more surface area means faster pickling.
  2. Toss: Combine cucumbers, rice vinegar, sugar, sesame oil, and salt in a bowl. Toss vigorously until the sugar and salt dissolve.
  3. Rest: Let sit on the counter while you cook your main dish (at least 10 minutes).
  4. Drain (optional): For a “dry” pickle, use a slotted spoon to serve. For extra tang, drizzle a little pickling liquid over your bowl.

Notes

  • Best eaten the same day for maximum crunch.
  • Great with rice bowls, grilled meats, or anything rich and savory.
