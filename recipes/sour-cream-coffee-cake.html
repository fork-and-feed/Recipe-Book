Sour Cream Coffee Cake

Classic tender coffee cake with a cinnamon-brown sugar nut swirl and crunchy topping.

← Back to Home
Prep: 15 min Bake: 45 min Total: 60 min Pan: 9x9 Serves: 9

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup butter or margarine, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp salt

Cinnamon-Nut Layer & Topping

  • 1/2 cup brown sugar, packed
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped nuts

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (180°C). Grease a 9x9-inch (22x22 cm) pan.
  2. Cream butter, sugar, and 1 egg together well. Beat in the second egg. Mix in baking soda and sour cream.
  3. In another bowl, mix flour, baking powder, and salt. Add to batter and stir just to combine.
  4. Spread 1/2 the batter into the prepared pan.
  5. Mix brown sugar, cinnamon, and nuts together. Sprinkle 1/2 over the batter.
  6. Spoon the remaining batter in small dollops over the top (it doesn’t need to cover perfectly). Sprinkle the remaining cinnamon mixture over all.
  7. Bake 45 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
  8. Serve warm.

Notes

  • If your nuts are large, chop them fairly fine so the layers sit nicely.
  • Let it cool 10–15 minutes for cleaner slices (still warm and cozy).
