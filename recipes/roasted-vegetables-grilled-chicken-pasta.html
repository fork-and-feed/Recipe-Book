Roasted Vegetables & Grilled Chicken Pasta

Sheet-pan roasted veggies + grilled chicken + lemon-Parmesan “pan-juice” sauce.

← Back to Home
Serves: 4 Total: 30–35 min Oven: 400°F / 200°C Pasta: 1 lb

Ingredients

The Roasted Base

  • 2 cups mushrooms (cremini or button), quartered
  • 2 cups cherry tomatoes
  • 1 large red bell pepper, chopped into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 medium red onion, cut into thin wedges
  • 4 cloves garlic, whole and unpeeled
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper

The Protein & Pasta

  • 2 large chicken breasts (about 1 lb)
  • 1 lb (500 g) short pasta (penne, rigatoni, or fusilli)

The Finish

  • 1/2 cup freshly grated Parmesan, divided
  • Juice of 1/2 lemon
  • 1/2 cup reserved pasta water (use as needed)
  • Optional: fresh parsley or basil for garnish

Instructions

  1. Roast the veggies: Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C). On a large sheet pan, toss mushrooms, tomatoes, pepper, onion, and unpeeled garlic with olive oil, oregano, salt, and pepper. Spread in a single layer. Roast 20–25 minutes until tomatoes burst and onions char.
  2. Grill the chicken: Season chicken with salt and pepper. Heat a grill pan or skillet over medium-high heat with a drizzle of oil. Cook 5–7 minutes per side (to 165°F / 74°C internal). Rest 5 minutes, then slice into strips.
  3. Boil the pasta: Cook pasta in heavily salted water according to package directions. Scoop out 1 cup pasta water before draining.
  4. The “sauce” base: Pick roasted garlic cloves off the pan. Squeeze the soft garlic from the skins into a large serving bowl. Mash with a fork and stir in a splash of olive oil.
  5. Combine: Add hot pasta, all roasted veggies (including pan juices), and sliced chicken to the bowl.
  6. Toss & serve: Add lemon juice and half the Parmesan. Splash in some reserved pasta water and toss vigorously until lightly saucy and glossy. Add more pasta water if needed. Top with remaining Parmesan and fresh herbs.

Notes

  • Don’t skip the pan juices — they’re the flavor base for the “sauce.”
  • Warm pasta + Parmesan + starchy water emulsifies into a light, creamy coating.
