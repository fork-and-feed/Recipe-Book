Ham and Cheese Omelet

A quick method for a tender interior, light browning, and perfectly melted cheese.

← Back to Home
Prep: 3 min Cook: 4 min Total: 7 min Serves: 1

Ingredients (1 omelet)

  • 2–3 eggs
  • 1–2 tbsp diced ham
  • 2–3 tbsp shredded cheese (cheddar, Swiss, or mozzarella)
  • 1 tbsp milk or water (optional, for a softer omelet)
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1 tsp butter or oil

Method

  1. Beat eggs: Whisk eggs with milk (if using), a pinch of salt, and pepper until fully combined.
  2. Heat pan: Use a small non-stick skillet over medium heat. Melt butter until it foams but doesn’t brown.
  3. Cook eggs: Pour in eggs. Let sit 10–15 seconds, then gently pull cooked edges toward the center while tilting the pan so uncooked egg flows outward.
  4. Add filling: When eggs are mostly set but still slightly glossy, sprinkle ham and cheese over one half.
  5. Fold: Fold omelet in half. Cook 30–60 seconds more so the cheese melts.
  6. Serve: Slide onto a plate and eat immediately.

Notes

  • Lower heat = more tender omelet. If it browns too fast, reduce to medium-low.
  • If using 3 eggs, you may want a slightly larger skillet (8–9").
