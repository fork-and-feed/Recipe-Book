Porc du Diable Strips

Quick maple-ketchup glaze with a sticky, dark finish.

← Back to Home
Prep: 10 min Marinate: 15–30 min Cook: 8–10 min Total: 25–40 min Serves: 3–4

Ingredients

The Ingredients

  • Protein: 1 pork tenderloin, sliced into 1/2-inch strips
  • The Glaze: 1/4 cup maple syrup, 1/4 cup ketchup, 2 garlic cloves (minced), 1 tbsp apple cider vinegar
  • The Fat: 6 tbsp oil (for marinade) + 2 tbsp oil (for frying)

Instructions

The 3-Step Process

  1. Marinate
    Whisk glaze ingredients with 6 tbsp oil. Toss with pork strips. Let sit 15–30 minutes (the vinegar + maple tenderize quickly).
  2. Sear
    Heat a skillet with 2 tbsp oil over medium-high heat. Add pork strips (drain excess marinade first). Cook 3–4 minutes until golden and no longer pink.
  3. Glaze
    Pour remaining marinade into the pan. Stir constantly 1–2 minutes until bubbling, thickened, and coating the pork in a sticky, dark glaze.

Notes

  • Drain excess marinade before searing to reduce splatter and help the pork brown properly.
  • Great over rice with a quick side of steamed broccoli or green beans.
