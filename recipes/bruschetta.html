Bruschetta

Juicy cherry tomatoes, garlic, basil, and balsamic—perfect on toasted baguette.

← Back to Home
Prep: 10 min Rest: 15–20 min Total: 25–30 min Serves: 6

Ingredients

  • 4 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp balsamic vinegar
  • 1/4 cup basil (or Italian spice mix)
  • 2 tsp crushed garlic
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 4 cups cherry tomatoes, halved or quartered

Instructions

  1. Halve or quarter the cherry tomatoes.
  2. In a bowl, toss tomatoes with olive oil, balsamic vinegar, garlic, and salt.
  3. Stir in fresh basil (or Italian spice mix).
  4. Let sit 15–20 minutes so flavors develop.
  5. Serve on toasted baguette slices.

Notes

  • For best texture, toast bread right before serving.
  • If using dried Italian seasoning, start with 1–2 tbsp and adjust to taste.
