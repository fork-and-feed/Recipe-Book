Healthy Grilled Chicken

Toasty ciabatta with grilled chicken, melty cheese, peppery arugula, and a quick Dijon mayo spread.

← Back to Home
Prep: 10 min Cook: 10 min Total: 20 min Makes: 2 sandwiches

Ingredients (2 sandwiches)

  • 2 ciabatta rolls, sliced
  • 1 large chicken breast, sliced horizontally
  • 1–2 tsp olive oil
  • Salt and black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp dried oregano or Italian seasoning

Cheese (choose one)

  • Fresh mozzarella, provolone, or mild white cheddar

Vegetables

  • 1–2 handfuls arugula
  • 1 small tomato, thinly sliced (optional)
  • Thin slices red onion (optional)

Mayo Spread

  • 2 tbsp mayonnaise
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 1/2 tsp lemon juice
  • Pinch of salt

Optional Additions

  • Roasted red peppers
  • Avocado slices
  • Balsamic glaze drizzle

Instructions

  1. Cook the chicken: Lightly coat chicken with olive oil. Season with salt, pepper, garlic powder, and oregano. Grill or pan-sear over medium heat for 4–5 minutes per side until cooked through. Rest 3 minutes, then slice.
  2. Toast the bread: Lightly toast the ciabatta until warm and slightly crisp.
  3. Make the spread: Mix mayonnaise, Dijon, lemon juice, and a pinch of salt. Spread on both sides of the bread.
  4. Assemble: Layer in this order: chicken → cheese → tomato (optional) → arugula → red onion (optional).
  5. Optional melt step: Warm the assembled sandwich in a skillet or sandwich press for 1–2 minutes to soften the cheese.

Tip

Toss the arugula with a few drops of olive oil and lemon juice before adding for extra flavor.
