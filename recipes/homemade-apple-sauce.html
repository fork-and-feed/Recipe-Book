Homemade Apple Sauce

Simple stovetop applesauce with cinnamon, lemon, and the perfect sweet-salt balance.

← Back to Home
Prep: 20 min Cook: 30 min Total: 50 min Yield: ~8 cups

Ingredients

  • 6 lb apples (after cored and peeled)
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup white sugar
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 4 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 cup water

Instructions

  1. Prep the apples: Core and peel the apples (then slice or chunk them so they cook evenly).
  2. Cook: Combine all ingredients in a large pot. Cover and simmer for 20–30 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the apples are very tender.
  3. Finish: Mash or blend to your desired consistency.

Pro-Tip

If you like it extra smooth, an immersion blender works wonders right in the pot. If you prefer it chunky, a potato masher is your best friend!

Notes

  • Taste at the end and adjust sweetness or cinnamon to your liking.
  • For a looser sauce, add a splash more water while simmering.
