Classic Beef Tacos

Weeknight tacos with saucy seasoned ground beef and all the classic toppings.

← Back to Home
Prep: 10 min Cook: 15 min Total: 25 min Serves: 4

Ingredients

The Meat

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1/2 cup beef broth or water
  • 1 tbsp tomato paste

The Spices

  • 1 tbsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp onion powder
  • 1/2 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1/2 tsp salt & black pepper

The Essentials

  • Taco shells or tortillas (hard or soft)
  • Shredded lettuce
  • Diced tomatoes
  • Shredded cheddar or Monterey Jack cheese
  • Sour cream
  • Guacamole

Instructions

  1. Brown the Beef
    In a large skillet over medium-high heat, cook ground beef until fully browned. Drain excess grease.
  2. Season
    Lower heat to medium. Stir in spice blend and tomato paste. Cook 1 minute, stirring.
  3. Simmer
    Pour in beef broth (or water). Simmer 5–7 minutes, stirring occasionally, until thick and saucy.
  4. Warm Shells
    Heat taco shells/tortillas according to package directions.
  5. Assemble
    Fill shells with beef. Top with cheese, lettuce, tomatoes, sour cream, and guacamole.

Notes

  • For extra flavor, toast the spices in the beef fat for 30 seconds before adding broth.
  • If it thickens too much, add a splash of broth or water to loosen.
