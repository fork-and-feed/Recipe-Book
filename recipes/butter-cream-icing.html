Butter Cream Icing

Classic shortening-based buttercream for frosting a 2-layer cake (from Mom).

← Back to Home
Yield: 2 layers Time: 10 min

Ingredients

  • 1 cup shortening
  • 1 lb icing sugar
  • 1/4 tsp coarse salt
  • A splash of lemon juice
  • Cream or milk (to reach desired consistency)

Quick Instructions

  1. Cream: Beat the shortening, coarse salt, and lemon juice together until smooth.
  2. Combine: Gradually beat in the icing sugar until well mixed and fluffy.
  3. Adjust: Add cream or milk, 1 tablespoon at a time, until it’s perfectly spreadable.

Notes

  • If it’s too sweet, add a tiny pinch more salt or an extra drop of lemon.
  • If it’s too soft, beat in a bit more icing sugar. Too stiff? Add more milk/cream.
