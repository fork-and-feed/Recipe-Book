Ninja Crepe Recipe

Quick blender crepes with a short rest for the smoothest batter and best flavor.

← Back to Home
Prep: 5 min Rest: 30 min Cook: 10 min Yield: ~8–10 crepes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup 1% milk (plus a splash more if too thick)
  • 3/4 cup all-purpose flour, sifted
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tbsp melted butter, slightly cooled
  • 2 tsp sugar
  • 3/4 tsp vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. Liquid first: Add milk, egg, vanilla, and melted butter into the Ninja cup first.
  2. Dry second: Add flour, sugar, and salt on top.
  3. The pulse: Blend for 15–20 seconds until completely smooth.
  4. The rest: Refrigerate at least 30 minutes (this removes the flour taste and improves texture).
  5. Consistency check: After resting, if batter is thicker than heavy cream, whisk in 1 tbsp milk.
  6. Cook: Heat a non-stick pan over medium. Lightly butter. Pour about 3 tbsp batter and swirl quickly. Cook 60 seconds on side one, then 30 seconds on side two.

Notes

  • First crepe is often a “tester” — adjust heat or add a tiny splash of milk if needed.
  • Stack cooked crepes on a plate and cover lightly to keep them soft.
