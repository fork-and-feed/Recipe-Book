Cherry Cheese Cake (Mom’s Recipe)

No-bake cherry cheesecake with a fluffy filling and classic graham crust.

← Back to Home
Prep: 15 min Cook: 0 min Chill: 3+ hrs Total: ~3 hrs 15 min Serves: 8

Ingredients

  • 1 block (250g) cream cheese, softened
  • 1/2 tub (500ml) Cool Whip
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 can cherry pie filling
  • 1 graham cracker pie crust

Instructions

  1. Whip softened cream cheese, sugar, and lemon juice until smooth.
  2. Gently fold in Cool Whip until well combined.
  3. Spread the filling evenly into the graham cracker crust.
  4. Top with cherry pie filling, spreading to the edges.
  5. Refrigerate until firm before serving (at least 3 hours is best).

Notes

  • For cleaner slices, chill overnight and wipe your knife between cuts.
  • Swap cherry filling for blueberry or strawberry if you want variety.
