Mexican Salad

Fresh romaine topped with a zesty lime-jalapeño bean mix (from Richard).

← Back to Home
Serves: 4–6 Prep: 15 min Marinate: 10–20 min

Ingredients

  • 1 head romaine lettuce
  • 1 can kidney beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cup corn
  • 1 cup scallions or green onions, finely chopped
  • 3 tomatoes, chopped and seeded
  • 1 avocado (to serve)

Dressing

  • 1/4 cup lime juice
  • 1/2 tsp sugar
  • 1/2 jalapeño, seeded and finely chopped
  • 1 tsp minced garlic
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, mix the kidney beans, corn, scallions, and chopped tomatoes.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the lime juice, sugar, jalapeño, garlic, and olive oil to make the dressing.
  3. Pour the dressing over the bean mixture and let it sit to marinate for 10–20 minutes.
  4. Coarsely chop the romaine and place it in a serving dish (or a large salad bowl).
  5. Spoon the marinated bean mixture over the lettuce.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste, and top with sliced avocado.

Notes

  • For extra crunch, add crushed tortilla chips right before serving.
  • If making ahead, keep the lettuce separate until serving so it stays crisp.
