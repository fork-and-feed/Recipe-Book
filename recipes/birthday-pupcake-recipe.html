The Birthday Pupcake Recipe

Simple banana–peanut butter pupcakes for a dog-friendly birthday treat.

← Back to Home
Prep: 10 min Bake: 20 min Total: 30 min Yields: ~6

Ingredients

  • 1 ripe banana, mashed
  • 3 tbsp peanut butter (must be xylitol-free)
  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 cup oats (or whole wheat flour)
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • Optional frosting: peanut butter

Instructions

  1. Preheat & prep: Heat oven to 180°C (350°F). Grease a muffin tin or use paper liners.
  2. Mix the wet: Mash banana until smooth. Stir in peanut butter and egg until well combined.
  3. Add the dry: Fold in oats (or flour) and baking powder.
  4. Bake: Scoop batter into the muffin tin. Bake 18–20 minutes, until a toothpick comes out clean.
  5. Cool & decorate: Cool completely before “frosting.” Hot treats can upset a pup’s stomach.

Safety Note

  • Xylitol is toxic to dogs. Always confirm the peanut butter label is xylitol-free.
