Black Pepper Chicken (Alcohol-Free & Seafood-Free)

A savory, peppery stir-fry with a glossy glaze.

← Back to Home
Prep: 15 min Cook: 10 min Total: 25 min Serves: 4

Ingredients

The Protein

  • 1 lb (450 g) boneless, skinless chicken thighs or breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces

The Produce

  • 1/2 yellow onion, sliced into chunks
  • 1 green bell pepper, sliced into chunks
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced into chunks
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tsp fresh ginger, grated
  • 2–3 green onions, sliced (keep white and green parts separate)

The Pantry & Sauce

  • 3 tbsp neutral oil (vegetable, canola, or grapeseed)
  • Alcohol-free “wine” sub: 2 tbsp chicken broth + 1/2 tsp rice vinegar
  • 2 1/2 tbsp soy sauce, divided (1/2 tbsp for marinade, 2 tbsp for sauce)
  • 1 tbsp hoisin sauce (seafood-free oyster sauce substitute)
  • 1 tsp Better Than Bouillon (roasted chicken base)
  • 5 tbsp warm water
  • 1 tbsp + 1 1/2 tsp cornstarch, divided (1 tbsp for marinade, 1 1/2 tsp for sauce)
  • 1/4 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp freshly cracked black pepper, plus more to taste
  • 1/2 tsp sesame oil (optional garnish)
  • Optional: 1/2 tsp dark soy sauce (for deeper color)

Preparation

  1. Prep the liquid sub: Mix 2 tbsp chicken broth and 1/2 tsp rice vinegar in a small bowl. Set aside.
  2. Marinate the chicken (10–15 min): In a bowl, combine chicken with: 1 tbsp of the broth/vinegar mix, 1/2 tbsp soy sauce, 1/2 tsp black pepper, 1/4 tsp baking soda, 1 tbsp cornstarch, and 1 tbsp oil. Mix well.
  3. Make the sauce: Whisk Better Than Bouillon into 5 tbsp warm water until dissolved. Whisk in hoisin, 2 tbsp soy sauce, the remaining 1 tbsp broth/vinegar mix, and 1 1/2 tsp cornstarch (and dark soy sauce if using).

Cooking Instructions

  1. Sear: Heat 2 tbsp oil in a large skillet or wok over high heat. Add chicken, spread it out, and sear for 2 minutes without moving. Toss until cooked through. Remove chicken and set aside.
  2. Aromatics: Add 1 tbsp oil to the pan. Stir-fry garlic, ginger, and the white parts of the green onions for 30 seconds.
  3. Veggies: Add onion and bell peppers. Stir-fry 1–2 minutes until slightly softened but still crisp.
  4. Glaze: Add chicken back to the pan. Stir the sauce (cornstarch settles!) and pour it in. Toss about 1 minute until bubbling and glossy.
  5. Finish: Turn off heat. Stir in green onion tops, sesame oil (if using), and extra black pepper to taste. Serve over hot rice.

Notes

  • High heat + short cook keeps the peppers crisp and the sauce glossy.
  • For extra pepper punch, add another 1/2 tsp cracked black pepper at the very end.
