Cream of Ramp Soup (Large Batch)

Big-batch creamy ramp soup with potatoes, garlic, and a smooth blended finish.

← Back to Home
Prep: 15 min Cook: 25 min Total: 40 min Serves: 8–10

Ingredients

  • Ramps: 750g (about 1 full freezer bag), rinsed and rough chopped
  • Potatoes: 7–8 large white potatoes (~2kg), cubed
  • Onions: 2 medium onions, chopped
  • Garlic: 5 tbsp freshly chopped garlic
  • Chicken broth: 7–8 cups (~1.75 litres)
  • Heavy cream: 4 cups (1 litre)
  • Seasoning: 2 tbsp salt + 2 tbsp pepper (plus to taste)

Instructions

  1. Sauté
    In a large stockpot, sauté onions and garlic until the onions are translucent.
  2. Simmer Potatoes
    Add chicken broth and potatoes. Cover and simmer 15 minutes until potatoes are soft.
  3. Add Ramps
    Stir in chopped ramps. Cover again and simmer 5 minutes.
  4. Incorporate Cream
    Add heavy cream, salt, and pepper.
  5. Blend
    Remove from heat. Use an immersion blender to blend until smooth and creamy.

Notes

  • If it’s too thick, add a splash of broth to loosen.
  • Taste at the end—ramp batches vary in strength, so adjust salt/pepper if needed.
