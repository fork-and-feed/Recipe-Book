Greek Lemon Potatoes

Braised-then-crisped roasted potato wedges with lemon, oregano, garlic, and olive oil.

← Back to Home
Prep: 5 min Marinate: 30 min (optional) Cook: ~35 min Oven: 400°F / 200°C

Ingredients

  • Potatoes: 4–5 small/medium (skin on), cut into quarters (about 1-inch thick)
  • Liquid: 1/2 cup broth + juice of 1 lemon
  • Fat: 1/4 cup olive oil
  • Seasoning: 3 cloves garlic, minced + 1 tbsp dried oregano + salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Toss: Put potato wedges in a roasting pan. Pour over olive oil, lemon juice, broth, garlic, and oregano. Add salt and pepper right before cooking.
  2. Braise (Step 1): Cover the pan tightly with foil. Bake at 200°C (400°F) for 20 minutes. This steams them until buttery soft.
  3. The blast (Step 2): Remove foil. If there’s a lot of liquid, spoon a little out (leave the oil). Increase heat to 230°C (450°F) or turn on the broiler.
  4. Crisp: Roast another 10–15 minutes without flipping until edges are blistered and golden brown.
  5. Serve: Let sit 2 minutes to soak up any remaining oil before serving.

Notes

  • Optional marinate: toss everything and let sit 30 minutes before baking for deeper flavor.
  • If you broil, keep a close eye — they go from golden to dark fast.
