Sheet-Pan Sausage + Veg (Cooked or Raw)

One-pan dinner with two timing paths: fully cooked sausage or raw sausage links.

← Back to Home
Prep: 10 min Cook: 20–30 min Total: 30–40 min Oven: 425°F / 220°C

Ingredients (Both Versions)

  • 3–4 cups broccoli florets
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2–3 tbsp oil
  • 1 tsp salt + 1/2 tsp pepper
  • 1–2 tsp garlic powder
  • Optional: paprika / chili flakes / Italian seasoning

Version A: Fully Cooked Sausage

Examples: kielbasa, smoked sausage, cooked Italian sausage (package usually says “fully cooked”).

Ingredients

  • 1 pack fully cooked sausage, sliced into 1/2-inch coins

Steps

  1. Heat oven: 425°F / 220°C. (Optional: preheat your sheet pan for better browning.)
  2. Season veg: Toss broccoli + pepper + onion with oil, salt, pepper, and garlic powder.
  3. Roast veg first: Spread in a single layer. Roast 10 minutes.
  4. Add cooked sausage: Stir veg, add sausage, spread out again.
  5. Finish roasting: Roast 10–15 minutes more until browned at the edges and everything is hot.
  6. Optional finish: Broil 1–2 minutes for extra crisping (watch closely).

Version B: Raw / Uncooked Sausage

Examples: raw Italian sausage links, raw bratwurst, fresh sausage.

Ingredients

  • 1 pack raw sausage (links or chunks)

Steps (Best Timing)

  1. Heat oven: 425°F / 220°C.
  2. Start sausage first (key): Put raw sausage on one side of a sheet pan (whole links are easiest). Roast 10 minutes.
  3. Add veg: Toss broccoli + pepper + onion with oil, salt, pepper, and garlic powder. Add veg to the other side of the pan (single layer as much as possible).
  4. Keep roasting: Roast 15–20 minutes more, flipping/stirring halfway, until:
    • Sausage is browned and cooked through (160°F / 71°C internal), and
    • Veg is tender with crispy edges.
  5. Optional finish: Broil 1–2 minutes at the end for extra browning.
