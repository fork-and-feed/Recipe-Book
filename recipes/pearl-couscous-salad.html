Pearl Couscous Salad

Toasted pearl couscous + lentils with a bright lemon-honey dressing and sweet-savory mix-ins.

← Back to Home
Prep: 25 min Cook: 25 min Chill: 8 hrs Total: ~8 hrs 50 min Serves: 6

Ingredients

The Grains & Legumes

  • 2 cups pearl couscous
  • 1/2 cup French lentils
  • 2 1/2 cups water (for couscous)
  • Water for boiling lentils, as needed

The Mix-ins

  • 2 Roma tomatoes, diced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 small cucumber, diced
  • 1/2 cup dried cranberries
  • 1/2 cup raisins
  • 1/3 cup diced red onion
  • 1/4 cup chopped chives
  • 2 tbsp chopped parsley
  • 1 green onion, thinly sliced

The Dressing & Seasoning

  • 5 tbsp olive oil, divided (1 tbsp for toasting + 1/4 cup for couscous)
  • Juice of 2 lemons
  • 2 tbsp honey
  • 1 tsp ground sumac
  • 2 tsp curry powder (optional)
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. Toast & Cook Couscous
    Heat 1 tbsp olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Add pearl couscous and stir constantly until toasted (about 3–4 minutes). Add 2 1/2 cups water and bring to a boil. Cook until tender (about 10 minutes). Rinse couscous, transfer to a bowl, and mix in 1/4 cup olive oil to prevent sticking.
  2. Cook Lentils
    Place lentils in a small pan with water. Bring to a boil, then simmer until tender (15–20 minutes). Drain and rinse, then add to the couscous.
  3. Combine
    Add tomatoes, cucumber, pepper, cranberries, raisins, red onion, chives, parsley, and green onion. Stir well.
  4. Dress & Chill
    In a separate bowl, whisk lemon juice, honey, sumac, salt, pepper, and curry powder (if using). Pour dressing over the salad and stir well.
  5. Rest
    Refrigerate for 8 hours before serving so flavors meld.

Pro Tip

  • Since this rests for 8 hours, the couscous and lentils absorb a lot of dressing. If it looks dry right before serving, add a squeeze of lemon or a tiny drizzle of olive oil to brighten it up.
