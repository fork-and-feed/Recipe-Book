Watermelon, Cucumber, and Avocado Salad

Fresh, juicy, and creamy—finished with feta, basil, and a lime-olive oil drizzle.

← Back to Home
Prep: 10 min Cook: 0 min Total: 10 min Serves: 4–6

Ingredients

  • 4 cups watermelon, cubed
  • 2 cups cucumber, diced
  • 1–2 medium avocados, cubed
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 tbsp lime juice
  • 1/2 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil, torn or chiffonade
  • Salt & pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil and lime juice to make the dressing.
  2. In a large salad bowl, combine watermelon, cucumber, and avocado.
  3. Drizzle dressing over the salad and toss gently to coat.
  4. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Top with crumbled feta and fresh basil just before serving.

Notes

  • For the best texture, serve immediately after adding avocado.
  • If making ahead, prep everything and add avocado + dressing right before serving.
