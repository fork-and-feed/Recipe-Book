Banana Bread

Classic moist banana bread with walnuts—optionally loaded with chocolate chips.

← Back to Home
Prep: 15 min Bake: ~60 min Total: ~75 min Oven: 350°F Yield: 1 loaf

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup (125 mL) butter or margarine, softened
  • 1 cup (250 mL) granulated sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup (250 mL) mashed ripe bananas (about 3 medium)
  • 2 cups (500 mL) all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp (5 mL) baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp (2 mL) baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp (2 mL) salt
  • 1 cup (250 mL) chopped walnuts
  • Optional: 3/4 cup (175 mL) semi-sweet chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (180°C). Grease a 9x5x3 inch (23x12x7 cm) loaf pan.
  2. In a large bowl, cream butter and sugar together.
  3. Add eggs one at a time, beating until smooth. Blend in mashed bananas.
  4. In a second bowl, stir together flour, baking soda, baking powder, salt, and walnuts.
  5. Add dry ingredients to banana mixture, stirring only until moistened. (If using, fold in chocolate chips.)
  6. Transfer batter to prepared loaf pan.
  7. Bake about 1 hour, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
  8. Let stand 10 minutes. Remove from pan and cool on a rack.
  9. Wrap to store.

Notes

  • For best flavor and texture, use bananas with lots of brown spots.
  • Don’t overmix once flour goes in—stir just until combined.
