Banana Chocolate Chip Muffins

Moist banana muffins studded with semisweet chocolate chips—quick, classic, and kid-friendly.

← Back to Home
Prep: 15 min Bake: 18–20 min Total: 35 min Oven: 350°F Yield: 12 muffins

Ingredients

  • 4 ripe bananas
  • 1/3 cup butter, melted
  • 1 egg
  • 2/3 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup semisweet chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease muffin tins (or line with paper liners).
  2. In a large bowl, smash peeled bananas and melted butter together with a fork until creamy.
  3. Add sugar, egg, and vanilla; mix just until combined.
  4. Stir in salt and baking soda.
  5. Add flour and chocolate chips. Mix until just combined—do not overmix.
  6. Divide batter into muffin tins.
  7. Bake 18–20 minutes, or until a toothpick comes out clean.

Notes

  • For taller muffins, fill cups about 3/4 full and don’t overmix the batter.
  • Bananas with lots of brown spots give the best flavor.
