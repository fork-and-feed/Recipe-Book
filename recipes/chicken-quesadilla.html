Chicken Quesadilla

Crispy golden tortillas stuffed with salsa chicken, sautéed peppers and onions, and melty cheese.

← Back to Home
Prep: 10 min Cook: 15 min Total: 25 min Serves: 4

Ingredients

  • 1 lb chicken breast or thighs, cut into small bite-sized pieces
  • 1/2 cup store-bought salsa
  • 2 cups shredded cheese (Mexican blend or Monterey Jack)
  • 4 large flour tortillas
  • 1/2 onion, diced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • Salt & pepper, to taste
  • Butter or oil, for frying

Instructions

  1. Sear Chicken
    Heat oil in a skillet over medium-high heat. Season chicken with salt, pepper, cumin, and chili powder. Cook 5–7 minutes until browned and just cooked through. Remove chicken and set aside.
  2. Sauté Veggies
    In the same pan, add onions and peppers. Sauté 3–4 minutes until softened and slightly charred.
  3. Combine & Simmer
    Lower the heat. Add chicken back to the pan with the salsa. Stir 1 minute to coat and warm through, then remove filling from heat.
  4. Prep Pan
    Wipe skillet clean. Melt a pat of butter over medium heat (or add a small slick of oil).
  5. Assemble
    Lay a tortilla flat. Sprinkle cheese over the surface. Spoon the salsa-chicken mixture over one half.
  6. Fold & Crisp
    Fold tortilla over. Cook 2–3 minutes per side until golden and crisp and cheese is bubbly.

Notes

  • If your salsa is very watery, simmer the filling 1–2 minutes longer so it doesn’t leak.
  • Cut into wedges and serve with extra salsa, sour cream, or guac.
