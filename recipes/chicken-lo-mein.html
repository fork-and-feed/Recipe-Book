Chicken Lo Mein

Tender marinated chicken, glossy sauce, and crisp-tender vegetables tossed with lo mein noodles.

← Back to Home
Serves: 3 Prep: 10–15 min Cook: 10 min Total: ~25 min

Ingredients (3 servings)

Noodles

  • 300 g lo mein noodles
  • 2 tbsp neutral oil (for stir-frying)

Chicken Marinade

  • 300 g chicken breast, thinly sliced
  • 1 1/2 tbsp light soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch
  • 1 tsp chicken bouillon paste (or ~3/4 tsp Better Than Bouillon)
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 1 tbsp Shaoxing wine (optional)

Sauce

  • 1 1/2 tbsp light soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 2 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp chicken bouillon paste
  • 2 tbsp water

Aromatics + Vegetables

  • 2 cloves garlic (~2 tsp minced)
  • 1 tbsp ginger, minced
  • 1/2 bunch Chinese broccoli (or broccolini)
  • 1/2 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1/2 red onion, sliced
  • 1 cup bean sprouts

Method

  1. Cook noodles: Boil until slightly underdone. Drain and set aside.
  2. Marinate chicken (10–15 min): Mix chicken with soy sauce, cornstarch, bouillon paste, sesame oil, and Shaoxing wine (optional). The cornstarch should form a light coating, not a thick paste.
  3. Make sauce: Whisk light soy sauce, oyster sauce, sugar, bouillon paste, and water.
  4. Cook chicken: Heat 1 tbsp oil on high heat. Sear chicken until lightly browned and just cooked through. Remove.
  5. Stir-fry vegetables: Add remaining oil. Cook garlic + ginger (10–15 seconds), then stir-fry vegetables until just tender.
  6. Combine: Add noodles + sauce and toss until glossy. Add chicken back in. Finish with bean sprouts and toss briefly.

Notes

  • Undercook noodles slightly so they don’t go mushy in the stir-fry.
  • High heat and quick tossing = better texture and less steaming.
