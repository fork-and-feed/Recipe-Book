Double-Breaded Chicken Schnitzel

Ultra-crispy schnitzel with a reinforced, double breadcrumb crust (minimalist shallow fry).

← Back to Home
Prep: 15 min Cook: 10 min Total: 25 min Serves: 4

Ingredients

  • 2 large chicken breasts (about 4 cutlets once butterflied)
  • Salt and black pepper
  • Plain flour (for dredging)
  • 2–3 eggs, whisked (for egg wash)
  • Fine breadcrumbs (for coating)
  • Neutral oil (for shallow frying)

Instructions

1) The Prep

  1. Butterfly the chicken breasts and pound them between plastic wrap until evenly thin (about 5 mm).
  2. Season both sides generously with salt and black pepper.

2) The 5-Step Breading Process

Set up three bowls: (1) plain flour, (2) whisked eggs, (3) fine breadcrumbs.

  1. Flour: Coat the chicken lightly and shake off excess.
  2. Egg: Submerge in the whisked egg wash.
  3. Breadcrumbs: Press firmly into fine breadcrumbs.
  4. Egg: Dip back into the egg wash for a second coating.
  5. Breadcrumbs: Final press into breadcrumbs to seal the double layer.

3) The Minimalist Shallow Fry

  1. Heat a skillet over medium heat. Add just enough neutral oil to thinly cover the bottom of the pan.
  2. When the oil is shimmering, lay the chicken in carefully (work in batches if needed).
  3. Fry 3–4 minutes per side until deep golden brown and cooked through.
  4. Drain on a wire rack or paper towel for 1 minute before serving.

Serving Ideas

  • Lemon wedges + simple salad
  • Potato wedges or mashed potatoes
  • Cucumber salad or quick slaw
