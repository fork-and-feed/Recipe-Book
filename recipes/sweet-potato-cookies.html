Sweet Potato Cookies

Soft, bakery-style cookies with sweet potato puree, chocolate chips, flax, and bran.

← Back to Home
Prep: 15 min Bake: 10–12 min Total: 30 min Oven: 350°F Yield: ~48 cookies

Ingredients

  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1 cup (2 sticks) margarine or butter
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup sweet potato puree
  • 1 1/2 tsp vanilla
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 12 oz semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • 1/4 cup ground flax seed
  • 1/4 cup ground bran

Instructions

  1. Cream the Base
    Place sugars and butter in a bowl and blend until creamy.
  2. Add Wet Ingredients
    Add the egg and vanilla; mix well. Gradually add sweet potato puree and mix until combined.
  3. Add Dry Ingredients
    Add flour, baking soda, salt, flax, and bran. Mix until a dough forms.
  4. Fold in Chips
    Add chocolate chips and mix until just combined.
  5. Bake
    Bake at 350°F for 10–12 minutes.
  6. Cool
    Cool on wire racks.

Notes

  • If your puree is very wet, chill the dough 20–30 minutes for thicker cookies.
  • Use rounded teaspoons for smaller cookies or a tablespoon scoop for bigger bakery-style cookies.
