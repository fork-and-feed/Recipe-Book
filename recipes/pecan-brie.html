Pecan Brie

Baked brie topped with quick candied pecans, maple, and cinnamon.

← Back to Home
Prep: 10 min Cook: 10 min Bake: 5–10 min Total: 25 min Serves: 6–8

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup pecan halves
  • 2 tbsp brown sugar
  • 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tbsp maple syrup
  • 1 brie wheel

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
  2. Remove the top rind of the brie.
  3. Sauté pecans, brown sugar, cinnamon, vanilla, and maple syrup in a pan until candied.
  4. Pour the candied mixture over the brie.
  5. Bake 5–10 minutes until the cheese is melted.

Quick Tip

  • Cut the top rind while the brie is very cold—slice off just a thin layer so the topping sinks in as it melts.
