Creamy Kraft Greek Marinade

The mayonnaise creates a “protective coat” that locks in juices and helps browning.

← Back to Home
Prep: 5 min Marinate: 30 min–4 hr Cook: 12–22 min Serves: 4

Ingredients

  • Chicken: 1.5–2 lbs chicken breasts (pounded to even thickness)
  • The Base: 3/4 cup Kraft Greek Dressing
  • The Secret: 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • Fresh Kick: 1 large clove garlic, minced
  • The Zing: Juice of 1/2 a fresh lemon

Instructions

  1. Whisk the Sauce
    In a bowl, whisk together the Greek dressing, mayonnaise, garlic, and lemon juice until smooth and creamy.
  2. Coat the Chicken
    Place chicken in a large Ziploc bag or bowl and pour the mixture over it. Make sure the chicken is fully “painted” in the creamy sauce.
  3. Marinate
    Chill in the fridge at least 30 minutes (up to 4 hours).
  4. Cook (choose one)
    • Grill: Medium heat. The mayo helps prevent sticking and encourages browning.
    • Oven: Bake at 400°F (200°C) for 18–22 minutes.
    • Air Fryer: 375°F for 12–15 minutes.

Why Mayo?

  • No sticking: Better than oil at keeping chicken from clinging to grill grates.
  • Even browning: Mayo proteins brown well, giving a better crust.
  • Juiciness: Acts like an insulator so lean breast meat doesn’t dry out as fast.
