Greek Rice (The “One Clove” Version)

Fast, tangy rice made with chicken broth and Greek dressing — big flavor from one garlic clove.

← Back to Home
Prep: 2 min Cook: 20 min Total: 22 min

Ingredients

  • Rice: 1 cup long-grain white rice
  • Liquid: 1.75 cups chicken broth
  • Flavor: 1/4 cup Kraft Greek Dressing
  • Aromatic: 1 large clove fresh garlic, minced
  • Fat (optional): 1 tbsp butter (helps keep grains separate)

Instructions

  1. Sauté
    Heat the butter (or a splash of the dressing) in a medium pot over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and cook for about 45 seconds—just until fragrant.
  2. Toast
    Stir in the dry rice and toast with the garlic for 1 minute. This adds a nutty flavor and helps prevent mushy rice.
  3. Liquids
    Pour in the chicken broth and the Greek dressing. Give it one quick stir.
  4. Simmer
    Bring to a boil, then immediately reduce heat to low. Cover with a tight lid.
  5. Steam
    Simmer for 15–18 minutes. Don’t lift the lid.
  6. Rest
    Remove from heat and let sit (covered) for 5 minutes. Fluff with a fork and serve.

Notes

  • If you want it extra tangy, add a tiny splash more dressing after fluffing.
  • For more bite, stir in lemon zest or chopped parsley at the end.
