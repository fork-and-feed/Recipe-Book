Slow-Cooker Chili

Bold spice blend • browned beef base • slow-simmered tomato richness

← Back to Home
Prep: 15 min Cook: 4 hr (High) Cook: 8 hr (Low) Serves: 6–8

Ingredients

  • 800g ground beef
  • 1 large onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can tomato paste
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 750 ml tomato sauce
  • 1 can diced tomatoes (800 ml)
  • 2 cans beans (800 ml total), drained and rinsed

The Bold Spice Blend

  • 4 tbsp chili powder
  • 1 tbsp cumin
  • 1 tbsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tbsp garlic powder
  • 2 tsp oregano
  • 1 tbsp brown sugar
  • 1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 1/2 tsp salt (adjust to taste)

Instructions

  1. Sear & Sauté
    Brown ground beef in a skillet over medium-high heat. Add onions and cook until soft. Stir in garlic and tomato paste; cook 2 minutes until the paste turns a deep rust color.
  2. Bloom Spices
    Add chili powder, cumin, smoked paprika, garlic powder, and oregano. Stir for 60 seconds until very fragrant.
  3. Transfer
    Pour beef broth into the pan to scrape up the flavorful bits, then transfer everything to the slow cooker.
  4. Slow Cook
    Stir in tomato sauce, diced tomatoes, brown sugar, salt, and Worcestershire. Cook on High for 4 hours (or Low for 8 hours).
  5. Finish
    Add drained/rinsed beans about 2 hours before serving to help them keep their texture.

Notes

  • For thicker chili, crack the lid for the last 30–60 minutes on High.
  • Great toppings: shredded cheese, sour cream, green onions, jalapeños, or tortilla chips.
