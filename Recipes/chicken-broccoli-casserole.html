Chicken & Broccoli (or Cauliflower) Casserole

A customized, creamy bake with a savory stock-based sauce.

← Back to Home
Prep: 25 min Bake: 25 min Total: ~50 min Serves: 6

Ingredients

The Base

  • Pasta: 375g (penne, fusilli, or rotini)
  • Vegetable: 1 large head broccoli or cauliflower, cut into florets
  • Protein: 1 lb chicken breast or thighs, diced
  • Cheese: 3 cups shredded cheese (cheddar or a blend), divided

The Sauce

  • Onion: 1 large, finely chopped
  • Garlic: 2 cloves, minced
  • Thickener: 4 1/2 tbsp cornstarch
  • Liquids: 2 cups chicken stock + 1 1/2 cups milk
  • Flavor: 2 tsp Dijon mustard
  • Seasoning: salt and black pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. Prep the Components
    Preheat oven to 425°F. Boil pasta in salted water according to package directions. Steam broccoli (or cauliflower) until just fork-tender.

    In a large pan, cook diced chicken with salt and pepper until golden and cooked through. Remove chicken and set aside.
  2. Build the Sauce
    In the same pan, sauté onion and garlic until softened and fragrant. Sprinkle cornstarch over onions and stir for 1 minute to coat.

    Slowly whisk in chicken stock, then whisk in milk. Simmer over medium heat, stirring constantly, until thickened. Whisk in Dijon and 1 1/2 cups (half) of the shredded cheese until melted and smooth.
  3. Combine & Bake
    Fold cooked pasta, steamed veggies, and chicken into the sauce until well-coated. Transfer to a 9x13-inch baking dish. Bake for 15 minutes.
  4. The Golden Topping
    Remove from oven and sprinkle remaining 1 1/2 cups cheese on top. Bake 10 minutes more until melted, bubbly, and golden.

Notes

  • If the sauce thickens too much before baking, add a splash of stock or milk to loosen.
  • For extra browning, broil 1–2 minutes at the end (watch closely).
