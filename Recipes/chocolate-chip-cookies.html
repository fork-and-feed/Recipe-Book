Chocolate Chip Cookies

Chewy in the middle, crisp at the edges.

← Back to Home
Prep: 15 min Cook: 10 min Total: 25 min Makes: 18 cookies

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup (113g) unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup (100g) brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup (50g) white sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 1/4 cups (155g) all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 cup (170g) chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment.
  2. Cream butter and sugars until fluffy. Beat in egg and vanilla.
  3. Mix flour, baking soda, and salt. Stir into wet ingredients.
  4. Fold in chocolate chips.
  5. Scoop 1–2 tbsp portions, spacing them apart.
  6. Bake 9–11 minutes until edges are set (centers will look slightly underbaked).
  7. Cool 5 minutes on the sheet, then move to a rack.

Notes

  • For thicker cookies, chill dough 30–60 minutes.
  • Swap in chopped dark chocolate for gooier pockets.
