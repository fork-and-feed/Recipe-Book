Honey Garlic Chicken or Shrimp

The wok-steam & cornstarch velvet method for a glossy honey-soy glaze.

← Back to Home
Prep: 10 min Velvet: 15–20 min Cook: 10 min Total: ~35–40 min Serves: 3–4

Ingredients

  • 1 lb chicken breast/thighs (sliced thin) or shrimp
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch (for velveting)
  • 1 tbsp water or oil (for velveting)
  • 4 tbsp honey
  • 4 tbsp soy sauce (divided: 1 tbsp for marinade, 3 tbsp for sauce)
  • 1 tsp garlic, minced
  • 1/2 tsp ground ginger
  • 1 tbsp cooking oil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. Velvet & Marinate
    Toss chicken (or shrimp) with cornstarch, water (or oil), and 1 tbsp soy sauce. Mix until the cornstarch is dissolved and protein is evenly coated. Rest 15–20 minutes.
  2. Prep Sauce
    Whisk honey with the remaining 3 tbsp soy sauce.
  3. Wok-Steam Broccoli
    Add ~1 inch of water to the wok. Add broccoli, cover, and steam 5 minutes. Remove broccoli, drain water, and wipe wok dry.
  4. Bloom Aromatics
    Heat cooking oil in the dry wok over medium heat. Add garlic and ginger; sauté 30 seconds until fragrant.
  5. Sear Protein
    Increase heat to medium-high and add velveted protein.

    For chicken: Let it sear 1 minute before stirring, then stir-fry until cooked through.
    For shrimp: Cook about 1 minute per side until pink.
  6. Glaze
    Pour in honey-soy mixture. The cornstarch from the protein will thicken it into a glossy glaze. Simmer 1 minute while tossing to coat.
  7. Final Toss
    Return broccoli to the wok and toss to coat. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Serve
    Serve over rice or rice noodles.

Notes

  • If the glaze gets too thick, loosen with a splash of water.
  • Add a squeeze of lime or a sprinkle of sesame seeds if you want a brighter finish.
