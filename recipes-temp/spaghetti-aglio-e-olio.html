Spaghetti Aglio e Olio

Fast garlic-and-olive-oil pasta with a little heat.

← Back to Home
Prep: 5 min Cook: 15 min Total: 20 min Serves: 2

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 4–6 cloves garlic, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 tsp red pepper flakes (more/less to taste)
  • Salt
  • Parsley, chopped (optional)
  • Grated parmesan (optional)

Instructions

  1. Cook spaghetti in salted water until al dente. Reserve 1/2 cup pasta water.
  2. Meanwhile, warm olive oil in a pan over medium-low heat. Add garlic and cook gently until lightly golden.
  3. Add red pepper flakes for 10–20 seconds.
  4. Toss in drained spaghetti with a splash of pasta water to emulsify into a light sauce.
  5. Season with salt. Finish with parsley and/or parmesan if you like.

Notes

  • Keep heat low so garlic doesn’t burn (burnt garlic tastes bitter).
  • Add lemon zest or sautéed shrimp for a variation.
